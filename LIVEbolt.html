LIVEbolt

Smart lock retrofit concept designed for renters. Removable, non-destructive, key-preserving, and focused on clean usability and thoughtful hardware design.

Overview

LIVEbolt is a smart lock retrofit concept created to address a common renter limitation: adding modern access control without permanently modifying existing hardware. The system preserves traditional key use while introducing app-based control through a compact and thoughtfully designed external module.

Tools

  • Concept development and sketching
  • Mechanical layout and packaging logic
  • User-centered design thinking
  • System-level concept planning

Highlights

  • Designed specifically for renters and temporary installations
  • Maintains compatibility with existing mechanical locks
  • Emphasis on clean installation and removal
  • Balanced usability, security, and aesthetics
