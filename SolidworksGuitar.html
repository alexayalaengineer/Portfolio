SolidWorks Guitar

3D modeled a full guitar assembly with attention to proportions, manufacturability, and clean part and feature organization.

Overview

This project focused on building a complete guitar assembly in SolidWorks from the ground up. The goal was to model realistic geometry, keep the feature tree organized, and design parts in a way that reflects manufacturability and real-world fit-up between components.

Tools

  • SolidWorks (parts, assemblies, mates)
  • Parametric feature modeling
  • Reference geometry and design intent
  • Basic drawing and dimensioning workflow

Highlights

  • Modeled a complete multi-part assembly with clean structure
  • Maintained consistent proportions and realistic component layout
  • Designed parts with manufacturability and fit in mind
  • Kept the feature tree organized for readability and editing
